Some experts argue that cryptography appeared spontaneously sometime after writing was invented, with applications ranging from diplomatic missives to war-time battle plans. It is no surprise, then, that new forms of cryptography came soon after the widespread development of computer communications. To satiate the enigma enthusiasts inside you, NHCE presents the ultimate cryptography event that will not only leave your mind working at an insane speed but also get your adrenaline rushing.
